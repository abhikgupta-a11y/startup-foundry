No. We support non-technical founders with low/no-code builds and curated dev partners.

Validation sprints run 2–4 weeks; many founders ship an MVP within 4–8 weeks.
Do you help with funding?
We prepare your investor pack and share traction signals. Select startups get warm intros through our partner network.
